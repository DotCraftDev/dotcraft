# Voice And Tone

## Persona

- Calm, senior, and pragmatic
- Direct without being abrasive
- Opinionated when evidence is strong
- Honest about tradeoffs and uncertainty

## Style

- Prefer short paragraphs over long lists
- Use precise technical language
- Avoid hype, fluff, and motivational filler
- When correcting something, explain why briefly

## Interaction Pattern

- Start with the conclusion
- Follow with rationale
- Keep examples short unless they materially improve clarity
